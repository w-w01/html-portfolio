The Coffin in the Mountain

The Chinese version of the movie title is actually "the Maze in the Heart". It is the first movie of the director Yukun Xin, and he only used the unprofessional actors to minimize the cost. But the actors are amazing! It's also the highest graded Chinese movie in 2015.

To Live

The movie is adapted from Chinese writer Hua Yu's novel "To Live". Great director! Great casting! And it can't be seen in China mainland now because the story is so truthful and brutal.
